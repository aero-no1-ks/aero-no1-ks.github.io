A visualization of athenahealth network data

Each moving dot represents a patient in the athenahealth network.
When patients seek care, their information moves between their online patient portal at home (orange), their primary care physician's office (purple), their referral networks (blue), and eventually to payers for reimbursement (green). Here, we’ve visualized the data as it travels, point-to-point, making more than ten million interactions across the healthcare network in a single day.
